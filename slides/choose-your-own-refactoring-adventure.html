Make Refactoring a Regular Part of Your Workflow

Refactoring

  • Changing the structure of existing code without changing its behavior
  • Supports the process of changes,
  • Makes it easier to modify and maintain in the future

Why We Dont Refactor

  • Team doesn't understand how
  • Long-term benifits aren't worth immediate effort
  • Overhead; youre paid to write new features
  • It might break something

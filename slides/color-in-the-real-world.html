    What is color

  • Wavelengths sensed by our eyes.
  • Infrared and lower wavelengths, Ultraviolet and the higher wavelengths

Subtractive vs Additive Color

Subtractive Color

  • Pigments absorbing light
  • CMYK
  • Print, E-paper

Additive Color

  • Emitted Lightbeams
  • RGB
  • screens

Color Blindness

  • 4% of humans have some color blindness. 1 out of 25 people
  • 8% men, 5% of women
  • Most common is low green sensing
  • Rare no or low red sending

Testing for Colorblindness: Photoshop

  • View > Proofseup > color blindness
  • Great way to test sites for readability!
  • Or test with js

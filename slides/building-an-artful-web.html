What is Art Anyway?

Art is temporal

  • Responsive design drama - it all looks the same. The same can be said for Victorian housing in SF.
  • Every work is born of its time.
  • We are both limited by and inspired by the technology that is available.
  • Andy Goldsworthy - a site specific sculptor - only collects materials from the site.
  • Limited by materials and space.

Rain Shadows

  • Andy Goldsworthy - goes outside, lays on the ground, waits for rain, then takes photo of the dry spot.
  • Time is his material
  • Patience is his tool
  • Technically, not challenging, but respected

Wolf Kahn

  • A landscape painter who escaped from Nazi Germany
  • Says: "Your work isn't messy enough"
  • Start broadly
  • The best way to learn is by making a mess
  • Then cleaning it up
  • This is why Sass exists
